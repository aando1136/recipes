Honey Oranges

Ingredients:
 - One whole Orange
 - One-Half cup of Honey

Tools
 - Breading Knife
 - Plastic or Glass bowl 

Preparation steps:
1) Using the breading knife, cut  the orange vertically into 1/2-inch wide slices
2) Place the slices into the bowl, taking care to make sure that no slice completely covers another.
3) Pour the honey into the bowl until all orange slices are submerged.
4) Place bowl in refrigerator for 2-3 hours
5) Remove the bowl from the refrigerator.
6) Consume at leisure.
